General Instruction:





  • When performing the strikes and blocks, stay in a stationary position (feet facing front).
  • Place the camera on the same height as your chin.
  • Adjust the camera angle 6 degrees facing down.
  • Stand 170 cm away from the camera.
  • You may choose to show or hide the skeleton, FPS, or angles from the Developer Options.
  • After, performing the poses, you may view each of your poses and its grades.
